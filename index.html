About us

Company with huge experience

Founded in 2010 in Europe, Venge was built on a passion for timeless design and exceptional craftsmanship. What began as a small studio creating bespoke furniture pieces has grown into a full-scale design and joinery company now based in Sydney, Australia.

Our philosophy is simple — to combine European aesthetics with modern Australian living.

Every project we create is unique, reflecting the personality and lifestyle of each client. From custom kitchens and wardrobes to complete interior solutions, we craft spaces that are functional, elegant, and built to last.
